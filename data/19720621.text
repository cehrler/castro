Thousands Say Farewell

East Berlin ADN International Service in German 0943 GMT 21 Jun 72 L

[Text] Berlin--Fidel Castro, first secretary of the Cuban Communist Party
Central Committee and premier of Cuba, left Berlin Wednesday. Many
thousands of Berliners bade Fidel Castro and his party-government
delegation a most cordial farewell. Thousands lined the road from the
guest's residence to the airport.
